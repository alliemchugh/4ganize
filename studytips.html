Study Tips
What would you like tips on today?
  • Make a study timetable
  • Put your phone away while studying
  • Close all tabs on computer that your are not using
  • Simplify your notes
  • Do not cram before a test! Take shorter but more study periods
  • Give yourself a certain amount of time to complete a task
  • Identify all the task you want to complete before you start
  • Do not multitask! Focus on one assignment at a time
  • Make sure you work in a clean environment I.E. your desk
  • Create flash cards
  • Quiz yourself and practice by teaching others.
  • Technology is great but trying writing something out
  • Color code topics to help your brain recall information
  • Repetition is the best way to remember something
  • Get enough sleep the night before the test
  • Read each question carefully
  • Do not stay on a question for to long. If you do not know the answer skip the question and come back to it.
  • After you have finished all the questions, go back through the test and check your answers
  • If a question is confusing to you, do not hesitate to ask teacher for clarification.
  • Highlight important terms in your notes
  • Color code your notes
  • Add visuals to your notes to help you better understand the material.
  • Take your notes in Blue ink instead of black ink because it helps you retain the information better
  • Keeps your notes concise! do not write information that is not necessary
  • Chewing gum can help you focus
  • Drinking water helps improves your mental performance
  • Try to improve your posture because it can help your memory and learning
  • Try to get at least 8 hours of sleep to help enhance your learning
  • Eat dark leafy greens that have a large amount of B6 and B12 which are associated with improvements in alertness and memory
